American Football

American football is a very popular sport mostly watched in the United States but cherished from parts of the whole world. There are different types of football leagues for any ages from 6 year old flag football to 40 year old NFL players. The sport gained inspiration from the old sport of rugby with a man named Walter Camp with his addition of specific rules and types of plays. The sport of football has been around since 1869 and is still on of the biggest all time watched games in the U.S. and other countries that love watching the entertaining sport.
