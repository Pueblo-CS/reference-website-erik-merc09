American Football

American football is a very popular sport mostly watched in the United States but cherished from parts of the whole world.There are different types of football leagues for any ages from 6 years old flag football to 40 year old NFL players. The sport gained inspiration from the old sport of rugby with a man named Walter Camp with his addition of specific rules and types of plays.
