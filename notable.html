Tom Brady

Tom Brady is a very popular player of American Football and is known by any fan or person thats ever watched football. Tom Brady is now retired and a commentator for football games and played the position of Quarterback for New England Patriots and Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Tom Brady played football for 23 seasons and in those 23 he has won 7 Superbowls which were superbowl 36, 38, 39, 49, 51, 53, and 55 against a big diversity of tough teams that earned their spot in the superbowl. Tom Brady will forever change the standard for football as one of the greatest of all time and has inspired so much people to be better, to play football, and to shoot for the top and never give up. This player will forever go down in history as the best and he always overcame other peoples judgement on him an proved everybody wrong about how a quarterback should be in the NFL.
