Rules of American Football

The goal of American Football is to finish the game with more points than the other team.

  • 4 downs every single play on offense
  • Every 10 yards a team resets their downs
  • Don't cross line of scrimmage before ball is snapped
  • Do not hit anyone from the back or blindside
  • When on offense you are not able to hold onto a player
  • When on defense you cannot hold someone from their neck collar
  • A touchdown counts as 6 points
  • Can get extra points after a touchdown by running the ball in (2 points) or kicking a field goal (1 point).
  • Can get 3 points from making a field goal kick from any place on the field.
  • Only 11 players allowed on the field from each team
  • Can't step out of bounds on the field
